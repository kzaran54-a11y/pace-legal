Questions

What do I get with Pace Plus?

The planner is free forever — your day, calendar, tasks, notes, journal, nutrition and Pulse never cost anything.

Plus unlocks the deep stuff: Month and Year recaps, month-vs-month nutrition, file attachments in shared notes, and unlimited collaborative notes. £1.99 a month or £14.99 a year, after a 7-day free trial.

I want a refund

Because Apple takes the payment, Apple issues the refunds — we have no way to do it from our side.

Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, find Pace, and choose Request a refund.

I got a new phone — where did my stuff go?

If you were signed in, sign in on the new phone and everything comes back.

If you used Pace as a guest, your data only ever lived on the old device — there was no cloud copy to restore. Sign in to keep a backup from now on.

How do I add a friend?

Open Socials → Add friend and send a request to their email address. Nothing is shared until they accept — a request on its own connects nothing.

What can my friends actually see?

Only what you choose to share, and never the details. A friend can see that you're busy at 2pm — never what the event is. Titles are never sent.

Location is off by default and always time-limited. Ghost mode makes you invisible and pauses every share at once.

Do you track me or sell my data?

No. Pace has no ads, no third-party analytics, and no tracking of any kind. We don't sell or share your data, and we never will.

Pace is local-first: your data lives on your phone, and only syncs to the cloud if you sign in. The full details are in the Privacy Policy.
